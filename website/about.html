About Us

At Sterling Tutors, we provide premium academic and wellbeing support for families in Kenya’s leading international and private schools, including International School of Kenya, Peponi House Preparatory School, Kenton College Preparatory School, St Andrew's School Turi, Banda School, Brookhouse School, Braeburn Garden Estate School, Pembroke House School, and Durham.

We offer personalised subject tutoring, homeschooling support, examination preparation, gym and fitness support, and supervised group activities including Karura walks and outdoor wellness sessions. Our aim is to provide families with trusted, high-quality support tailored to children who deserve nothing but the very best academically, physically, and personally.

Sterling Consultancy supports international, diplomatic, NGO, and relocating families in navigating Kenya’s leading British and international curriculum schools. With extensive experience working alongside top IAPS, ISEB, IGCSE, and IB schools, we help parents identify the best educational pathway for their child based on academic ability, sporting talent, creativity, personality, and long-term goals. From school selection and relocation guidance to personalised educational planning, we provide trusted insight and tailored support for families seeking the very best opportunities in Kenya’s international education sector.
