Sterling Consultancy

Relocating to Kenya and unsure which school is the right fit for your child? Sterling Consultancy supports international families, NGO staff, diplomatic and UN families, and parents relocating from countries such as Spain, Brazil, the UK, the UAE, South Africa, and beyond.

Kenya is home to some of Africa’s leading British and international curriculum schools, offering pathways including IAPS, ISEB, IGCSE, IB, and A Levels. However, choosing the right school can be overwhelming, especially when considering your child’s personality, academic strengths, sporting ambitions, artistic talents, learning support needs, or long-term university goals.

At Sterling Consultancy, we help families make informed educational decisions with confidence.

Whether your child is:

  • academically exceptional,
  • highly creative or artistic,
  • passionate about sport,
  • preparing for top university pathways,
  • or simply needs the right environment to thrive,

our team will guide you through the most suitable schools, pathways, and opportunities available in Kenya.

While we are not agents for any school, our extensive experience working within leading international schools and supporting families from those communities gives us valuable insight into school culture, academic expectations, co-curricular strengths, boarding environments, and admissions processes.
